Bill of Materials

- Circuit board: After brd file in this repository

On top of this moderboard the following components are mounted:
- Moderboard: Arduino Pro Mini 328 - 5V/16MHz
- DC-DC converter: 5V to 3.3V
- Sound chip: WT588D
- Accelerometer: GY-61
- Power MOSFET: 3 pcs 14n05
- Diode: 2 pcs 1n4148
- Resistor: 1pcs 568ohm
- Vibration sensor: SW18010P
